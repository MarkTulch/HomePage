  1. Began College

    University of Maryland, College Park

    I got accepted into college! I entered UMD as an Undeclared major (Letters & Sciences), with the intention to become a Marketing major within the next two years. I filled my courseload with accounting, economics, and business classes.

    It was the creative design aspect that I was hungry for; the idea of being able to create things as a profession seemed amazing.

  2. Tell-A-Terp

    University of Maryland, College Park

    As my first job out of high school, I worked at UMD's fund raising call center for my first semester of college. They mostly hired students, so I showed up to the interview wearing a full suit and tie, VANs sticking out beneath my dress pants, and skateboard clutched in hand.

    Most students just wore jeans and a T-Shirt.

  3. E.R.A. Intern

    National Institutes of Health

    This was my first real internship. I spent 3 months adjusting to the corporate world, learning how business processes and development cycles were applied to a large-scale operation. I worked with a small team of interns to test, verify, and report upon over 400 bugs affecting NIH systems.

    The real importance of this internship was my introduction to code. I learned to love seeing how editing pieces of code could affect the display and functionality of an application. It was like playing with legos, professionally.
